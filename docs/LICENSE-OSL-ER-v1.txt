-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This policy aims to:
1. Encourage open scientific use, replication, and peer review
2. Promote responsible application of research findings
3. Discourage harmful applications

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PERMITTED USE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You may use, copy, modify, and redistribute these materials for:

- Scientific research and replication studies
- Education and teaching
- Peer review and academic critique
- Open dissemination with proper attribution
- Commercial applications that benefit society
- Integration into AI/ML systems for beneficial purposes

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. ATTRIBUTION
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You must:
- Credit the author(s) and cite the original publication
- Include the DOI when referencing this work
- Cite the companion package (LRD v5) when appropriate

See CITATION.cff for proper citation format.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. ETHICAL GUIDELINES
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We ask that you DO NOT use these materials to:

- Develop systems intended to cause harm to people
- Support harassment, stalking, or discrimination
- Facilitate unauthorized surveillance or exploitation
- Create tools primarily designed for deception or coercion
- Manipulate financial markets in ways that harm others
- Undermine democratic institutions or processes

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. AI AND AUTOMATED SYSTEMS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If integrating this research into AI/ML systems:

- Consider the potential for both beneficial and harmful applications
- Implement appropriate safeguards against misuse
- Be transparent about the limitations of LRD-based predictions
- Do not overstate the predictive power of these methods
- Consider the impact on market stability and fairness

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. FINANCIAL APPLICATIONS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If using for financial applications:

- This research demonstrates statistical properties, not trading signals
- Past patterns do not guarantee future behavior
- Markets are complex adaptive systems with regime changes
- Use appropriate risk management and diversification
- Consider the social impact of algorithmic trading strategies
